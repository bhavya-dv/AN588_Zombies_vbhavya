Question 1: Calculating the population means and standard deviations.

var() and sd() commands as these are for samples. Therefore, I made a function stdev() to calculate standard deviation of the population, using population mean and n (from length(d$variable))

#writing a function for population standard deviation of variable y 
stdev <- function(y) {
  sqrt(sum((y - mean(y))^2)/length(y))}
